A Safe and Nurturing Home for Every Child

At Comfort Ark Haven, we provide specialist residential care where children can heal, grow, and thrive in a supportive environment tailored to their unique needs.

What We Do

Comfort Ark Haven is dedicated to offering high-quality residential care for children and young people who need a safe, stable, and caring environment. Our experienced team works closely with each child to understand their individual circumstances, helping them build confidence, develop essential life skills, and achieve their full potential.

We create warm, family-like settings where children feel valued and supported. Through evidence-based approaches and a trauma-informed care model, we address emotional, educational, and social needs. Every child in our care receives personalized attention, structured routines, and opportunities to form positive relationships that will serve them throughout their lives.

Our Homes

We operate welcoming residential homes in carefully selected locations, each designed to provide comfort, security, and a sense of belonging.

Willow House

A peaceful home in a quiet residential area, Willow House offers a calming environment with access to local schools, parks, and community facilities. Our dedicated staff team provides round-the-clock support in a setting that feels like home.

  • Capacity: Up to 4 young people
  • Ages: 11-17 years
  • Experienced care team
  • Individual bedrooms

Meadow Lodge

Set in a tranquil neighborhood, Meadow Lodge provides a nurturing space where children can develop independence while receiving consistent care. The home features comfortable living areas and a welcoming atmosphere that encourages personal growth.

  • Capacity: Up to 3 young people
  • Ages: 12-18 years
  • Therapeutic support available
  • Spacious communal areas

Referral Process

We make the referral process as smooth as possible to ensure children receive the care they need without delay.

1

Initial Contact

Reach out to our team by phone or email. We'll discuss the child's needs and circumstances to determine if we can provide appropriate support.

2

Assessment & Planning

We'll review all relevant information and arrange meetings with key stakeholders. Our team will assess how we can best meet the child's individual needs.

3

Matching & Introduction

Once a placement is agreed, we carefully match the child with the most suitable home. We arrange introductory visits to ensure comfort and compatibility.

4

Transition & Settling In

We support the child through their transition with a tailored plan. Our team maintains close communication with all parties to ensure a smooth start.
